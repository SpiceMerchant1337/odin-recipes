Pasta

This classic pasta is delicious with juicy Italian tomatoes. Layer the sauce with some garlic and butter, then stir with seasoned pasta! Enjoy this delicious meal with you loved ones, be sure to add in a side from our other recipes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta, a pack
  • Crushed tomatoes, two tbsp
  • Crushed garlic, one glove
  • Butter, 15g
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a well seasoned water according to its package instructions.
  2. Melt the butter and add in the garlic, cook until fragnant.
  3. Add the tomatoes along with preferred toppings and stir until cooked.
  4. To assemble, add in the pasta and mix until done.

Cheeseburger

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • %40 fatty ground beef, 200g
  • Cheddar cheese, two slices
  • Crushed garlic, one glove
  • Egg yolk, one
  • Vegs to your taste

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt&pepper, add the egg and the crushed garlic and smash into a patty.
  2. Preheat your iron pan.
  3. Cook the patty for two minutes on each side without oil.
  4. Turn off the heat and add the slice of cheddar on top, close the lid for 30 seconds.
  5. Cool for 5 minutes before serving it in your burger bun.
  6. At last, decorate with your preferred tops
