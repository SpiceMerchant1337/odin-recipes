Odin Recipes

  • Lasagna

    This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

    Ingredients

    • Lean ground beef, 500g
    • Crushed tomatoes, one can
    • Crushed garlic, two gloves
    • Egg, one

    Steps

    1. In a Dutch oven, cook the ground beef,garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in the can of tomatoes.
    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    4. To assemble, pour the egg into the hot ground beef mix and add the pasta on layers.
    5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, remove the foil and bake an additional 25 minutes.
