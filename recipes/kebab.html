Turkish style kebabs

This classic Turkish dish is delicious, famous and mouth watering! Great for weekend grills with mediterranean salads on the side. Fun for the whole pack!

Ingredients

  • %40 fat ground beef, 500g
  • Onion, two or three, to taste
  • Crushed garlic, two gloves
  • Egg yolk, one
  • Plain yoghurt, 200g

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt&pepper, add the egg and form the kebabs on a stick.
  2. Preheat your grill, season with onion.
  3. Cook the kebab meat for two minutes on each side, check regularly.
  4. Crush the garlic and mix well with the plain yoghurt, add any herbs to taste.
  5. Optional: Wrap in a Turkish lavash bread with leftover onions or add tomato sauce.
